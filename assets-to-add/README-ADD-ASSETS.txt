FOLDER: images/
  ├── hero-poster.jpg
  │     A single still frame from the hero video (or a matching
  │     photo). Shown while the video is loading, and on devices
  │     that cannot autoplay video.
  │     Recommended: same aspect ratio as the video (16:9).
  │
  └── hero.jpg
        A full-width landscape photo of your farm / fields / product.
        Used as a CSS background fallback if video fails entirely.
        Recommended: at least 1920px wide.
